Where to Eat


Pizza Express Union Square/Street

Welcome to Pizza Express Gluten-Free Pizzeria! We are proud to offer a wide range of delicious, high-quality pizzas that cater to those with gluten sensitivities or preferences. Our mission is to ensure that everyone can enjoy the classic pizza experience, regardless of dietary restrictions.

Our gluten-free pizzas are crafted with the same love and attention as all our dishes. We use a specially formulated gluten-free dough, topped with our signature tomato sauce and a variety of fresh, flavorful toppings. From classic Margherita to adventurous gourmet combinations, there is a pizza to satisfy every palate. In addition to our pizzas, we also offer a selection of gluten-free salads, pastas, and desserts, so you can enjoy a complete meal that suits your dietary needs.

Amarone

Ciao! Ready for a slice of cosmopolitan Italy? Vibrant with chatter, the clink of glasses and the shake-a-shake-a-shake of cocktails from the central cocktail bar, Amarone has all the buzz of a restaurant in central Milan.

If you can resist the temptation of crisp pizza straight from the central oven, freshly made pastas filled with classic Italian combinations might turn your head. Need some more time to decide? Dunk warm homemade focaccia in rich beef ragu and take your time making your mind up.

Cafe Andaluz

Hola, Amigos! Welcome to Cafe Andaluz, the little slice of Spain nestled in the heart of Aberdeen at 5 Bon-Accord Street. We've been dishing out sunshine and smiles since 2002, and we're not stopping anytime soon!

We're open from 12:00 to 22:00 or 22:30, depending on the day. That's right, we're here for you longer than your last holiday romance!

Now, let's talk about gluten. We understand that dietary requirements can be a challenge, which is why we've created a gluten-free menu that doesn't compromise on taste. From gluten-free beer to dessert and even paella, we've got you covered. However, please note that we're not a dedicated gluten-free facility, so if you have any concerns, don't hesitate to get in touch.
