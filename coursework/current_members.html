Hannes Berger CEO

Hannes Berger is the founder and Manager of Our Celiac Society. You might know him as Ceo of the biggest gluten-free company Schar.

He stepped down from his position to spread avearnes and to help celiacs in Aberdeen. To achieve his goal he started this society.

Gordon Ramsey Head Chef

Gordon Ramsey Michelin Star restaurant owner came here to help us create amazing Gluten-Free recipes so our members can enjoy Gluten-Free life.

Gordon Ramsey is also Our personal cook for all our events. You can ask him anything about Gluten-Free cooking at our events
